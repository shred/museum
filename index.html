Shred's Hardware Museum

Welcome to my private hardware museum. Here you will find all kind of computer related gadgets of the 1980s and 1990s, but also a few other rare or unique machines.

The main purpose is to have a catalog of my collection, and keep track of the history of each exhibit. But maybe you will also enjoy a virtual visit of my museum, and remember the good old times when computers have started to become a part of our households.
