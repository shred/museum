Atari 1040-STF

Configuration

  • Built: November 1988
  • Manufacturer: Atari Taiwan Manufacturing Corporation
  • Mainboard: C070789-001 Rev. D1
  • CPU: TMP68000 (8 MHz)
  • RAM: 1MB
  • OS: TOS v1.04 ("Rainbow TOS")
  • Floppy: Centuriontech GOEX drive for Atari ST, with OLED display
  • Centuriontech USBee r2022 Premium (gold plated contacts)

Trivia

  • Had an intact warranty seal and a protective film on the Atari badge when purchased.

Restauration Works

  • Cleaned the case and keyboard.
  • Original PSU (Mitsumi SR98) replaced with MeanWell RD-35A. New mains wires from switch to PSU. New ground wire from case to PSU.
  • Replaced all electrolytic capacitors with Vishay and Panasonic ones. One bi-polar axial cap was replaced with a radial one because I couldn't find an axial replacement.
  • Added four sockets for the ROM area.
  • Opened holes to solder in a socket for a Blitter chip.
  • Replaced original floppy disk with GOEX disk drive emulator.

Known Issues

  • PLCC socket for the blitter needs to be soldered in. Maybe I will also add a Blitter chip if I should find a cheap one.
