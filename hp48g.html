Hewlett Packard 48G

I'm a big fan of all kind of scientific calculators. Back in 1991, two of my schoolmates got the predecessor model, a HP 48S. I was amazed by the mathematical abilities of this machine. However at that time it was out of my budget, and I also owned two very capable Casio calculators that were more than sufficient for my school purposes.

In 2022, I finally let the dream come true and bought this HP-48G at an online auction platform.

The calculator is in an excellent condition. Except of some very tiny scratches on the IR interface cover, it looks pristine.

Configuration

  • Built: 1997 (5th week) in Singapore
  • Display: LCD, 131 x 64 pixels, monochrome (blue on light green), graphics
  • RAM: 32KB (not extensible)
  • ROM: Version HP48-R
  • With pouch and manual

Restauration Works

  • Carefully cleaned the case
  • Polished some minor scratches on the IR interface cover
