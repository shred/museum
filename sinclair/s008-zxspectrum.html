Sinclair ZX Spectrum

Status: Refurbished and working

Configuration

  • Built: Early 1983
  • Mainboard: Issue Two
  • CPU: Z80A
  • ULA: 5C112E (date code 8304, probably made in Phillipines)
  • RAM: 48KB
  • Came with an original PSU with EU plug (cleaned, untested, unrefurbished)

Trivia

  • I bought it in a bundle with a second ZX Spectrum, two Interface1, and a RAMTurbo joystick extension.
  • It had a security sticker, and an inventory label with a date of March 19th, 1985.

Restauration Works

  • Restauration was carried out between February and March 2022.
  • Thorough cleaning of the case. I also removed a security sticker that had been attached by the previous owner. It was very difficult to remove, and unfortunately left a lot of scratchmarks on the bottom shell.
  • The keyboard membrane connector was broken due to old age, and was replaced.
  • The faceplate got bent on my attempt to remove it, and was replaced with a new replica one.
  • Replaced two upper RAM chips that were reported as faulty.
  • Replaced 7805 with Traco Power TSR-1 DC/DC converter, removed heatsink
  • Recapping, Composite Mod
  • Calibrated video output

Known Issues

  • Output signal has a yellow tint even after calibration. Colors are completely broken. This may be due to the ULA type 5.
  • Bottom shell of the case has very visible scratchmarks from an old security sticker.

History

  • 2023-03-05:
    • Replaced LM1889 and recalibrated output. The yellow tint is a bit better now, but the colors are still wrong (e.g. green is looking like blue).
  • 2022-03-13: Calibrated video output
  • 2022-03-05
    • Replaced two broken upper RAM chips
    • Replaced 7805 with Traco Power, removed heatsink
    • Recapping, composite mod
  • 2022-02-21: Date of purchase
