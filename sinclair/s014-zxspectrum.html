Sinclair ZX Spectrum

Status: Refurbished and working

Configuration

  • Built: End of 1984, Assembled in Portugal
  • Mainboard: Issue 6A
  • CPU: Z80A
  • ULA: 6C001E-7 (date code 8435, probably made in Phillipines)
  • RAM: 48KB

Trivia

  • This machine was "Assembled in Portugal".
  • Was sold as "untested", but turned out to be completely dead. A membrane connector was broken off, and the previous owner tried to forcefully remove the keyboard faceplate.

Restauration Works

  • Restauration was carried out in March 2022.
  • All voltages were missing. Replaced 7805 by a Traco Power TSR1-2450. System worked again after that.
  • Recapping, Composite Mod.
  • Case cleaned, making sure that the "Assembled in Portugal" label was kept intact.
  • Membrane connector was broken off and missing, so the membrane was replaced.
  • Faceplate was heavily bent, and was replaced with a new "metallic red" one.

History

  • 2022-04-02
    • Replaced rubber feet (some were missing)
  • 2022-03-26
    • Replaced 7805 with Traco Power DC/DC converter
    • Recapping
    • Cleaned the case
    • New keyboard membrane, as the connectors of the old one were broken off.
    • Original faceplate was bent. Replaced it with a new "metallic red" one.
    • Composite mod
