Casio PRW-1300T

Status: Working

A digital watch with barometer, altimeter, compass, and temperature sensor. This model receives the German DCF77 longwave time code, so it does not need any manual time adjustment. The internal battery is charged by solar power. This model is equipped with a titane watchband.

This module 3070 was released March 2007.

Trivia

  • This watch still has its original battery, and I actually never had to manually adjust the time.
