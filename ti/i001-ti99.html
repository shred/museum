Texas Instruments TI-99/4A

Configuration

  • Built: 1983 (CW 37)

Trivia

  • Was sold as untested. It came with a speech synthesizer, TV modulator, and game cartridges. The bundle also contained a Poppy 9015 game console.
  • Game cartridges:
    • Beginning Grammar
    • Blasto
    • Buck Rogers
    • Espial
    • Indoor Soccer
    • Othello
    • Parsec
    • The Attack
    • TI Invaders
    • TI Extended BASIC

Restauration Works

  • The bosses that hold the transformer are broken due to age and mechanical overload. I printed four sleeves to close and stabilize them. The transformer is now held firmly in place again.

History
