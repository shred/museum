Sharp IQ-8300M

Status: Confirmed broken

Configuration

  • Built: Unknown, first half of the 1990s
  • 240 x 64 pixel dot matrix monochrome LCD
  • CPU: SC-62015
  • RAM: 128KB
  • IQ-703A "8-Language Translator"

Trivia

  • The Wizard organizer series was called IQ in Europe, but OZ in the USA.
  • This device came with a working 8 language translator card (English, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Swedish, Japanese, Chinese).

Known Issues

  • Working for a few minutes with fresh batteries, but then the system starts to deteriorate and eventually freezes.
  • Needs cleaning. However, cleaning the outer shell is difficult because IPA seems to dissolve the surface.
