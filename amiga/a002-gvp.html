GVP Impact Series II

Configuration

  • Built: around 1991/1992
  • HD: SCSI2SD v5
  • RAM: 4MB
  • ROM: Latest GVP faast V4.15

Trivia

  • Was delivered with a Fujitsu 54MB SCSI HD and 2MB of RAM. The HD was still working when I refurbished the extension.

Restauration Works

  • Cleaned and whitened the case top
  • Removed flash rust from metal case frame
  • Upgraded the firmware to the latest version found on the internet
  • Changed the internal power cable from 5.25" type to 3.5" type
  • New SCSI ribbon cable
  • Replaced HD with a SCSI2SD v5
  • Extended RAM from 2MB to the maximum of 4MB
  • Modified the board so it is powered by the Amiga, and does not need a separate PSU any more
  • Removed the case fan. It was never active because of its loud noise, and now it was colliding with the SCSI2SD board.
  • Replaced all case screws. Many original had damaged heads, one was missing.

History

  • 2021-10-30
    • Replaced overly bright LEDs with standard ones (blue and orange)
    • Fixed stability issue by replacing the SCSI wire
    • Removed the fan (was colliding with the SCSI2SD frame)
  • 2021-08-23
    • Mounted SCSI2SD drive
  • 2021-08-18
    • Polished flash rust from bottom frame
    • Added two more SIMMs, extending the memory to full 4MB
    • Replaced original 5¼" power connector with 3½" power connector
    • New power and HDD LEDs (blue and orange), but they are too bright
  • 2021-08-15: Upper shell returned from whitening
  • 2021-08-14
    • Modified to source power from the A500 instead of external PSU
    • Update to final firmware V4.15
  • 2021-08-05: Upper shell sent in for cleaning and whitening
  • 1994-04-01: Retired from active use
  • 1992-05-02: Date of purchase
