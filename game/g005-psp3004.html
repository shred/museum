Sony PSP-3004

Status: Working, but needs overhaul

Configuration

  • Built: Fourth quarter of 2008 (date code 8D)
  • PlayStation Portable 3004 (original color: black)
  • Motherboard: TA-090v2 or TA-090v3 (Brite 3000, Generation 3)
  • CPU: MIPS R4000, up to 333 MHz
  • RAM: 64 MB (main) + 4 MB (graphics)
  • Display: 480 x 272 Pixel, True Color
  • 32 GB MicroSD via MemoryStick Pro Duo adapter

Known Issues

  • WiFi switch lever is broken off on the SMD part. I cannot repair this with my equipment. The risk of accidentally unsolder other 0201 SMD parts around it is too high, and it would be close to impossible to resolder them. Since the PSP only supports the outdated WPA standard, it's not useful any more anyway.
  • MemoryStick cannot be read.

History

  • 2022-11-26
    • Reassembled the system into a clear black replica case. Unfortunately the replica case already came with a lot of tiny scratches on the backside.
    • Moved the LCD of the other PSP-3000 into this device.
  • 2022-11-13: Disassembled
    • Original case was missing the back frame with WiFi switch, and the battery cover.
  • 2022-10-09: Date of purchase
