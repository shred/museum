8BCraft RetroStone 2 Pro

Status: Working

Configuration

  • Built in 2020
  • RetroStone 2 Pro, limited "Clear Blue" edition
  • OS: Retrorangepi
  • Display: 3.5" IPS LCD, 640x480
  • CPU: Allwinner A20 (ARM Cortex-A7 dual core)
  • RAM: 1GB
  • Flash: 8GB internal + 64GB MicroSD, internal SATA m.2 connector
  • Battery: 4000mAh

Trivia

  • The "Clear Blue" case is a limited edition exclusively for Kickstarter backers. According to Pierre-Louis Boyer, only about 500 devices with this color have ever been produced.

History

  • 2020-02-26: Delivered
  • 2019-08-28: Backed on Kickstarter
