Nintendo Game Boy

Status: Working

Configuration

  • Built probably in early 1995
  • Game Boy "Play it loud!" edition, black case
  • Display: 2.6" STN dot matrix LCD, 160 x 144 pixels, 4 shades of "grey" (actually greenish), no backlight
  • RAM: 8KB + 8KB Display
  • CPU: Sharp LR35902 (Z80 inspired, but not binary compatible)
  • 4 Channel Stereo Sound (2 pulse wave, 1 PCM, 1 noise)

Trivia

  • I bought it in July 1995 during a holiday trip to Tenerife. I lost interest in it shortly after the holiday, so the overall condition is almost pristine.
  • It's a Game Boy of the "Play it loud!" edition that came with colorful cases. I wavered between a black and transparent case, and eventually decided for the black one. Maybe it was a good choice, as the transparent cases get yellow over the decades.
